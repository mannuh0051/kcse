How to Check KCSE Results

  • Step 1: Click "Check Results" Button

    Click the blue "Check Results" button in the top right corner of this portal.

  • Step 2: Enter Your Examination Details

    Enter your full name and KCSE index number in the verification form.

  • Step 3: Get USSD Verification Code

    You will receive USSD code (*811*842#) to use on your mobile phone for secure verification.

  • Step 4: Dial & Access Results

    Dial *811*842# on your phone to complete verification and access your KCSE grades.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What information do I need to check my KCSE results?

    You need your full name as registered during KCSE and your index number. The system will guide you through verification.

  • Why do I need to dial *811*842# for verification?

    USSD verification ensures the security of your results and confirms your identity using your registered mobile number.

  • Is the USSD service available 24/7?

    Yes, the USSD verification service (*811*842#) is available 24 hours a day on all mobile networks in Kenya.

  • How is my personal information protected?

    Your information is encrypted and protected according to KNEC data protection policies. It's only used for verification.
